Our Story

Founded in 2002 in a garage in Shmoville, SHMOBILE set out to answer one question: "Can you SHMO me now?" The answer, as it turned out, was yes.

What started as a college project that got wildly out of hand, SHMOBILE quickly grew into the world's most fictional wireless carrier. Armed with nothing but a dream, three soldering irons, and a suspiciously large supply of red plastic, our founders built the first SHMO prototype — a phone so durable, it survived being thrown at a wall during the investor pitch.

Today, SHMOBILE serves literally dozens of customers across at least 3 metropolitan areas. Our commitment to providing adequate wireless service at questionable prices remains as strong as our phones (which is very strong — seriously, try breaking one).

Our Mission

To put a SHMO in every pocket, whether they asked for one or not. We believe that everyone deserves access to a phone that can double as a self-defense weapon, a doorstop, and occasionally, a communication device.
